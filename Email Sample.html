Important Note

Many campus auxiliaries and services are open for the summer session. Please remember all students, employees, and visitors will be required to wear a mask in order to receive campus services and participate in indoor activities. Please maintain physical distancing and wash your hands or use the hand sanitizer provided.

BYUI Wellness Center is Open - Zoom Consultations Available

The Wellness Center is open and running in the Hart room 174. They are open Monday - Thursday, 9:00 am - 1:00 pm. Their services have resumed as normal, with the additional option for Zoom consultations for those who are not in Rexburg at this time. You can call the Wellness Center during their hours to schedule an appointment at: 208-496-7491.


Visit the BYU-Idaho Wellness Center website for additional information about Wellness Center services, and follow them on Instagram.

Reserve Fall Semester Essentials with Student Express

Student Express is a one-stop location for essentials to start your semester off right. Reserve your I-ard, textbooks, and parking permit online and skip the hassle and lines on move-in days! You can reserve all the items offered or just one – get only what you need. We will gather your reservations in one place for a quick and easy pick up. Your reservations will be available in Manwaring Center 176A - just past the bowling alley.

Fall Parking Permits
Fall parking permits are available online. Request your permit now and pick it up through Student Express from September 11th - 12th and September 14th - 15th.


For more information or to request your permit, visit the Parking Services website.
